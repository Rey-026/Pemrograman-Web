Popular Districts & Food

Popular Districts

Shibuya

Famous for the world's busiest pedestrian crossing. Great for shopping and nightlife.

Asakusa

The oldest part of Tokyo. Home to the beautiful Senso-ji temple and traditional shops.

Akihabara

The tech and anime capital of the world. Packed with electronics shops and manga stores.

Tips for Visiting Akihabara

Best visited on a Sunday when the main street closes to cars and becomes a pedestrian zone.

Famous Tokyo Food

  • Ramen – Tokyo-style soy-based broth with thin noodles.
  • Sushi – From high-end omakase to budget conveyor belt restaurants.
  • Tempura – Lightly battered, crispy-fried seafood and vegetables.
